Wait, But Why?

  • Many public behaviours require us to trust strangers
  • We have Rule of Law based on this trust
  • If there is no C at first, then everybody does a D
  • That is the source of the Dilemma
  • As Artists and Designers, and Creators, we need to practice our craft that in such a way as to induce niceness…
  • So what will you make? A movie? An ad? A public sign-board? A street play? An illustration? A “spatial arrangement”? A policy or a law?
