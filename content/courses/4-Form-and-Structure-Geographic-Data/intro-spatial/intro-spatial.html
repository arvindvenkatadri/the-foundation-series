Non-Fiction Time!

Kandinsky was remarkable for many things, but perhaps most so in that he wrote an entire book about why he painted, called the Über das Geistige in der Kunst: Insbesondere in der Malerei (Concerning the Spiritual in Art: Especially in Painting). This book, in German, is almost like a “credo”.

Since we can’t read German, let us read parts of an Article about Kandinsky’s credo: Kandinsky, Math Artist? by Karl Kattchee, Proceedings of Bridges 2013: Mathematics, Music, Art, Architecture, Culture (2013), Pages 473–476 Short Papers
